Pepperoni Pizza

Description!

Quick, Easy and Delicious-Homemade pizza crust and tomato sauce has never been easier-and this recipe for pepperoni pizza produces a delicious classic!

Ingredients

For the mass

  • 400g of wheat flour
  • 50g cornmeal
  • 250c.c. of warm water
  • 50c.c. sunflower or olive oil
  • 7g yeast
  • Wheat semolina
  • Sal c / n

For the Sauce

  • 1 can of crushed tomato
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried basil
  • Sal c / n

To Cover

  • 250g grated mozzarella
  • 150g pepperoni
  • Dried oregano

steps to follow

Preparation of the dough

  1. Place the wheat flour in a bowl along with the cornmeal
  2. Spread the yeast on one edge and the salt on the other without touching.
  3. Add the olive or sunflower oil and the warm water.
  4. Mix with your hands and knead for several minutes until you get a smooth dough
  5. Make a bun and let it sit covered for 60 minutes or until doubled in volume in a warm place away from drafts.
  6. Spread the work surface with wheat semolina and roll the dough out with a rolling pin until you achieve a circle the size of the pizza maker you are going to use to cook the Domino’s-style pizza
  7. Grease the pizza maker with oil and place the stretched pizza dough there . Mark the edges and pour a thread of oil on the surface of the dough
  8. Preheat the oven to 200º C.

Preparation of the sauce

  1. Place a Chinese strainer over a bowl and add the crushed tomato . Let it sit for 15 minutes so that the liquid drains off and is much more consistent
  2. Pour the liquid and pour the tomato into the container. Add the spices to taste and mix.

Assembling and firing

  1. Sprinkle the pizza dough with a little cheese and top with the sauce in the form of a string.
  2. Finish adding mozzarella cheese to pizza and pepperoni slices.
  3. Bake for 20 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Remove, cut into portions and drizzle a little olive oil
